Introduction

DriftCash ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile personal finance application.

Your Money, Your Device, Your Rules

  • 100% local storage — nothing ever leaves your phone
  • No servers, no cloud, no backups created by us
  • No analytics, no tracking, no data selling
  • Receipt photos & OCR processed on-device only
  • Uninstall = everything gone instantly

We built DriftCash for people who take privacy seriously.

Data Collection & Storage

All data stays on your device. We never upload anything.

We store locally:
  • Transaction records (amount, date, category, description, receipts)
  • Budget information
  • Categories & preferences
  • App settings (theme, currency, notifications)

Camera & Photo Library Access

Used only for receipt scanning:

  • Images are processed locally with on-device OCR (Google ML Kit)
  • Receipt photos are saved only on your device
  • No images are ever uploaded

Permissions Required

  • Camera → Scan receipts
  • Photo Library → Import existing receipt images
  • Notifications → Budget alerts & reminders

Data Processing & Third-Party Services

All processing happens on-device. The only third-party library used is Google ML Kit — and nothing is sent to Google. All OCR runs locally.

Data Security

  • Secure local storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences where applicable)
  • Your device’s lock screen & biometrics protect everything
  • Uninstall the app → all data is permanently deleted

We Do NOT

  • Share or sell your data
  • Upload anything to servers
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Collect personal identifiers
  • Create cloud backups

Your Rights

  • Full control — delete everything instantly
  • Clear data via Settings anytime
  • Uninstall = total data removal

Children’s Privacy

DriftCash does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Compliance

We comply with GDPR, CCPA, Apple App Store, and Google Play privacy requirements.
