Introduction

WiFi Ranger ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our WiFi signal monitoring service.

TL;DR – Quick Summary

  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • We DON'T send anything to servers
  • We DON'T track you
  • We DON'T sell your data (because we don't have it!)
  • Location permission is only for WiFi scanning (required by Android/iOS), not GPS tracking
  • You can delete everything anytime

WiFi Ranger is a privacy-first app. What happens on your device, stays on your device.

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

WiFi Ranger collects the following information locally on your device only:

1. WiFi Signal Data
  • WiFi network name (SSID)
  • Signal strength (RSSI) measurements
  • Connection status
  • Network speed test results (download/upload speeds)
2. Settings and Preferences
  • Scan interval preferences
  • Haptic feedback settings
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
3. Historical Data
  • Signal strength history (stored locally for up to 60 seconds)
  • Exported CSV files (when you choose to export)

How We Use This Information

All data collected by WiFi Ranger is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Used solely to provide you with WiFi signal monitoring and analysis features

Data Storage • Third-Party Services • Permissions

All data is stored securely on your device using platform-standard mechanisms (SharedPreferences/UserDefaults). Historical data is kept only in memory for live charts.

Speed Test (Cloudflare)

  • We use public endpoints at speed.cloudflare.com
  • No personal data is sent during tests

No Analytics • No Tracking

  • Zero analytics services
  • No usage tracking
  • No personally identifiable information collected

Required Permissions (Android & iOS)

Location permission is required by the OS to access WiFi info — we do not track your physical location.

Important: WiFi Ranger does NOT collect, store, or transmit your GPS or location data — ever.

Data Export • Children’s Privacy • Security

  • Export signal history as CSV anytime
  • Not directed to children under 13
  • All data protected by your device’s security (encryption, biometrics, etc.)

Your Rights

  • Clear all data in Settings
  • Delete the app → everything is gone
  • Export your data anytime
  • Use the app 100% anonymously
