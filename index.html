Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

Introduction

WiFi Ranger ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our WiFi signal monitoring service.

TL;DR - Quick Summary

  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • We DON'T send anything to servers
  • We DON'T track you
  • We DON'T sell your data (because we don't have it!)
  • Location permission is only for WiFi scanning (required by Android/iOS), not GPS tracking
  • You can delete everything anytime

WiFi Ranger is a privacy-first app. What happens on your device, stays on your device. ðŸ”’

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

WiFi Ranger collects the following information locally on your device:

1. WiFi Signal Data
  • WiFi network name (SSID)
  • Signal strength (RSSI) measurements
  • Connection status
  • Network speed test results (download/upload speeds)
2. Settings and Preferences
  • Scan interval preferences
  • Haptic feedback settings
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
3. Historical Data
  • Signal strength history (stored locally for up to 60 seconds)
  • Exported CSV files (when you choose to export)

How We Use This Information

All data collected by WiFi Ranger is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Used solely to provide you with WiFi signal monitoring and analysis features

Data Storage

  • All WiFi signal data and settings are stored locally using your device's secure storage mechanisms
  • Historical signal data is kept in memory for real-time charting (60-second window)
  • Settings preferences are saved using SharedPreferences (Android) or UserDefaults (iOS)
  • You can clear all historical data at any time through the app's Settings screen

Third-Party Services

WiFi Ranger uses the following third-party services for functionality:

Speed Test Services

  • We use Cloudflare's public speed test endpoints (speed.cloudflare.com) to measure your internet speeds
  • No personal information is transmitted during speed tests
  • Speed test data is only stored locally on your device

No Analytics or Tracking

  • WiFi Ranger does NOT use analytics services
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information

Permissions

WiFi Ranger requires the following permissions to function:

Android

  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To read WiFi connection information
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check network connectivity
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION or ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Required by Android to access WiFi information (we do not track your physical location)
  • INTERNET: To perform speed tests
  • VIBRATE: For haptic feedback (optional)

iOS

  • Location Services: Required by iOS to access WiFi information (we do not track your physical location)
  • Network Access: To perform speed tests
Important: While location permissions are required by Android and iOS to access WiFi information, WiFi Ranger does NOT collect, store, or transmit your physical location data.

Data Export

  • You can export your signal history as a CSV file at any time
  • Exported files are saved to your device's downloads/documents folder
  • You have full control over these files and can delete them whenever you wish

Children's Privacy

WiFi Ranger does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences and does not require age verification as it collects no personal information.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using platform-standard secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures (encryption, biometric locks, etc.)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all historical data through the app's Settings screen
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Export your data as CSV files
  • Use the app without creating an account or providing personal information

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and in our GitHub repository
