Privacy Policy

Introduction

DriftCash ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our financial tracking services.

TL;DR - Quick Summary

  • All your data stays on YOUR device
  • We DON'T send anything to servers
  • We DON'T track you
  • We DON'T sell your data (because we don't have it!)
  • You can delete everything anytime

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

DriftCash collects the following information locally on your device:

1. Financial Data
  • Transaction records
  • Account balances
  • Budget information
  • Expense categories
2. Settings and Preferences
  • Currency preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
3. Historical Data
  • Transaction history
  • Spending trends
  • Exported reports (when you choose to export)

How We Use This Information

All data collected by DriftCash is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Used solely to provide you with financial tracking and analysis features

Data Storage

  • All financial data and settings are stored locally using your device's secure storage mechanisms
  • Settings preferences are saved using SharedPreferences (Android) or UserDefaults (iOS)
  • You can clear all data at any time through the app's Settings screen

Third-Party Services

No Analytics or Tracking

  • DriftCash does NOT use analytics services
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do NOT integrate with any third-party financial services

Permissions

DriftCash requires minimal permissions to function:

Android

  • STORAGE: To save and export financial reports (optional)
  • VIBRATE: For haptic feedback (optional)

iOS

  • Storage Access: To save and export financial reports (optional)

Data Export

  • You can export your financial data as CSV or PDF files at any time
  • Exported files are saved to your device's downloads/documents folder
  • You have full control over these files and can delete them whenever you wish

Children's Privacy

DriftCash does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences and does not require age verification as it collects no personal information that is transmitted off-device.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using platform-standard secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures (encryption, biometric locks, etc.)
  • We recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all data through the app's Settings screen
  • Delete the app and all associated data at any time
  • Export your data as CSV or PDF files
  • Use the app without creating an account or providing personal information

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
